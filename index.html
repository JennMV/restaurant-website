Directions

Bits n' Bytes is located on the second floor of the T building beside the connecting bridge to J building. Can be easily found through the back doors of the Market Place Cafeteria, up the staircase to J building, then take a right down the J building hall and over the bridge to your left into T builbing.

The Bits n’ Bytes area has high tops and seating overseeing the student courtyard for a realaxing quiet space to enjoy your meal.
